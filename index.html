Roadracer Tools

Online tools for Motorcycle roadracers

Amateur roadracing is awesome, but there's a lot of room for improvement. Dissemination of grids and results, evaluation and access to laptimes -- all this needs a paint stroke from modern technology and internet software. While extremely modest at this point, roadracertools.com takes aim at these problems and tries to improve the experience!

After The Race
(aka atr.tools)

After The Race (atr.tools) is a full-fledged lap time evaluation tool. Create an account, upload your race or track sessions, and evaluate your performance over time. View your best and average laps by track and date.

Check out After The Race

Roadracer Results
Google Chrome Extension

Motolaptimes.com is an awesome resource for CCS/ASRA racers. But the content is very segregated and it's difficult to view a race's results across riders. This Google Chrome browser extension enhances motolaptimes.com and shows all riders laps together, as well as some cool lap plots!
